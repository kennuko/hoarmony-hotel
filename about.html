harmony hotel

On the left Wing, the hotel has 68 standard rooms of 5 different types /Single Rooms, Twin Rooms, Sheba Queens, Royal Kings and Emperor Suits/, a dining room with an extensive local and international cuisines buffet.
The right Wing of Harmony Hotel has 82 rooms with different types /Sheba Queen, Royal King, Emperor Suite, Standard Twin, Furnished Deluxe, Junior Presidential Suite and Presidential Suite/, fully central air conditioner, bank services, in house satellite TV and interactive TV system with paid movies, halls that could accommodate from 15-700 people for meetings, weddings, banquets and other functions.
Ras sport bar that offers panoramic views of Addis Ababa with a full range of beverages, light meal menu and varieties of entertainments.
Wellness Center with swimming pool, gym, sauna, steam, Moroccan bath, full-fledged beauty salon & massage services in it, business center, coffee shop and wireless internet access.
Safety of guests is also given the highest priority in the hotel. All hotel rooms equipped with fire sensors besides the extension of the hotel is well equipped with modern sprinkler system that could automatically extinguish fire. Above all Harmony Hotel is well known by the guests for its charming and supportive employees who always strive for the comfort and satisfaction of their guests. .
