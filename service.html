Harmony Hotel is a four-star hotel located in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia
It was established by Gollagul Trading and began operations on November 8, 2008
The hotel has undergone expansions, increasing its capacity to 150 rooms and 172 beds. It offers a range of amenities, including a spa, fitness center, swimming pool, restaurant, and business facilities
